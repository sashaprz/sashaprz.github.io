Hi! Nice to meet you! :)

I'm Sasha, a 15 year old based near Toronto, Canada. Right now, I'm an innovator at TKS, which has been awesome so far.

Ever since I was little, I've loved learning and building things. That eventually evolved into material science, which is the study of the materials that make up our world and how to optimize them.

So many technical breakthroughs depend on new materials being discovered, so there is huge potential for the field. I'm also really interested in batteries, but I'm still exploring the huge space of material science!

Other things I love are cross country, skiing, reading, and biking.
